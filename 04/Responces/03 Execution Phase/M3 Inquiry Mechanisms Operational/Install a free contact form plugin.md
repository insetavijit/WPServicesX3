Install a free contact form plugin

- No paid upgrades  

- No external CRM or automation integrations  

Create a single contact form with:

- Name  

- Phone or Email  

- Message field  

4. Configure form submission delivery to the approved inbox.

Submit a test form entry and verify successful message receipt.

---
## Execution

Step 1. Log in to the WordPress admin panel and wait until the dashboard is fully visible.

Step 2. Click Plugins, click Add New, type WPForms in the search field, locate WPForms – Contact Form by WPForms, click Install Now, wait for installation to complete, and click Activate.

Step 3. Click WPForms in the left-hand admin menu and click Add New.

Step 4. Enter a form name and select the Simple Contact Form template.

Step 5. In the form builder, verify the presence of the Name and Message fields.

Step 6. Add or adjust a field so that exactly one field is used for Phone or Email.

Step 7. Remove any other fields so that only Name, Phone or Email, and Message remain.

Step 8. Arrange the fields in the order Name, Phone or Email, and Message.

Step 9. Click Save to store the form.

Step 10. Click Settings, click Notifications, and enter the approved inbox email address in the Send To Email Address field.

Step 11. Verify no additional recipients or integrations are enabled and click Save.

Step 12. Click Pages, open the page titled Contact, and click Edit.

Step 13. Insert the WPForms block and select the newly created contact form.

Step 14. Click Update or Publish and wait for confirmation.

Step 15. Open the Contact page on the live site, fill in all form fields with test data, and click Submit.

Step 16. Open the approved inbox and verify that the test submission has been received.
