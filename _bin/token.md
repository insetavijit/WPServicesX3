## **Business Overview**

I own a local footwear business called **UrbanStep Shoes**, based in Kolkata, India.  
We design and sell **casual shoes for men and women**, focusing on comfort, durability, and everyday wear.

Currently, sales happen through:

- Offline retail store
    
- Instagram and WhatsApp inquiries
    

We do **not** yet have a professional website.

---

## **Website Objective**

The purpose of this website is to:

- Build brand credibility
    
- Showcase our shoe collections
    
- Allow customers to contact us easily
    
- Prepare the foundation for future online selling
    

This website is **not an e-commerce store yet**.  
(No online payments required at this stage.)

---

## **Primary Goal (Most Important)**

Generate **customer inquiries** via:

- WhatsApp click-to-chat
    
- Contact form
    

Secondary goal:

- Help customers explore shoe styles before visiting the store
    

---

## **Target Audience**

- Local customers (city & nearby areas)
    
- Men and women aged 18–45
    
- Casual footwear buyers
    
- Instagram users discovering the brand
    

Most visitors will:

- Use mobile phones
    
- Be first-time visitors
    
- Want quick visual browsing
    

---

- Clean, modern, fashion-oriented design
    
- Neutral tones (white, black, earthy colors)
    
- Large product images
    
- Simple typography
    
- No heavy animations
    

The site should feel:

- Trustworthy
    
- Stylish but simple
    
- Local yet professional
