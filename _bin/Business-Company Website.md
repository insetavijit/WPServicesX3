# **Business / Company Website Doctrine**

**Service Classification: Professional Business Presence & Lead-Generation System**

## **1. Service Definition (What This Service Actually Is)**

This service delivers a **professional, business-grade WordPress website** designed to establish credibility, communicate services clearly, and convert visitors into leads or inquiries.

It is **not** a personal blog or a design demo.  
It is a **digital business asset** that functions as the first trust signal for customers, partners, and prospects.

The website is built to support:

- Brand credibility
    
- Clear service presentation
    
- Lead capture and communication
    
- Long-term maintainability
    

## **2. Core Business Objectives**

The service exists to achieve **five primary outcomes**:

1. **Instant Trust & Professional Perception**  
    Visitors must immediately recognize the business as legitimate and credible.
    
2. **Clear Value Communication**  
    Services, offerings, and differentiators must be understood within seconds.
    
3. **Lead & Inquiry Generation**  
    The site must convert visitors into calls, messages, or form submissions.
    
4. **Mobile-First Accessibility**  
    The site must perform and display correctly on all common devices.
    
5. **Owner Control & Independence**  
    The business owner must manage content without developer dependency.
    

## **3. What It Includes (Deliverables)**

- Custom or semi-custom UI/UX design aligned with brand identity
    
- Responsive layout for mobile, tablet, and desktop
    
- Essential business pages:
    
    - Home
        
    - About
        
    - Services
        
    - Contact
        
    - Legal (Privacy / Terms, if required)
        
- Page builder or Gutenberg configuration
    
- Lead capture forms (contact, inquiry, WhatsApp integration)
    
- Basic CRM / email / analytics integration (if required)
    
- Basic SEO setup (URLs, metadata, indexing readiness)
    
- Performance-aware configuration (clean build, fast loading)
    
- Security hardening & deployment setup
    
- Admin access, documentation, and handover walkthrough
