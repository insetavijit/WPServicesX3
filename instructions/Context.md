UrbanStep Shoes is a local footwear brand based in Kolkata, India, specializing in comfortable, durable casual shoes for men and women. The business currently operates through an offline retail store and Instagram/WhatsApp inquiries, without a formal website presence.

The objective of this project is to design and deploy a **non-ecommerce, mobile-first WordPress website** that establishes brand credibility, visually showcases shoe collections, and generates customer inquiries through WhatsApp and a contact form. Online payments and cart functionality are explicitly excluded at this stage.

The website will serve as a **trust and discovery platform**, enabling first-time, mobile users (ages 18â€“45) to quickly browse products, understand the brand, locate the store, and initiate contact. Core pages include Home, About, Collections (showcase only), Store Location, Contact, and Privacy Policy.

The build will prioritize **clarity, speed, responsive design, and ease of administration**, using WordPress on shared hosting with Gutenberg or Elementor and free plugins where possible. The structure will be intentionally future-ready, allowing a smooth transition to WooCommerce and online selling when the business expands.

This project represents a practical example of **local business website execution focused on inquiry-driven conversion rather than e-commerce complexity**.
