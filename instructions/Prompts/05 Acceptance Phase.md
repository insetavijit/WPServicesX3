### **5. Acceptance Phase**

- **What**  
    Obtain formal confirmation that all validated deliverables satisfy the agreed requirements and formally close the project. Do not perform further execution after acceptance.
    
- **Scope**
    
    - **Final approval only**  
        Limit this phase to confirmation of completion against validated deliverables. Exclude new work, refinements, or change requests.
        
    - **Closure boundary**  
        Declare the project closed upon acceptance. Any work requested after closure constitutes a new engagement.
        
- **Reference**
    
    - **Client sign-off**  
        Secure written confirmation that all acceptance criteria have been met. Verbal approval is insufficient.
        
    - **Handover documents**  
        Transfer all agreed files, access credentials, documentation, and usage instructions. Exclude training or support beyond what was approved.
        
    - **Closure statement**  
        Issue a formal declaration confirming project completion and closure. This statement marks the end of responsibility under the current scope.
